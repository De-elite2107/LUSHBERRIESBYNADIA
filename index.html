About Us

Lushberries started as a small passion project in 2022. Our goal is to craft unique, refreshing mocktails that everyone can enjoy. Whether you're looking for something fruity, tangy, or just plain fun, we've got you covered.

We believe that a good drink is more than just taste—it’s an experience. Every ingredient is carefully chosen to create the perfect balance of flavor and style.

Our Menu

Here’s a taste of what we offer:

  • Tropical Twist – Pineapple, coconut, and passionfruit blended to perfection.
  • Citrus Spark – A refreshing burst of orange, lemon, and lime.
